About Us

We are a grass roots non-profit tech company / coding school offering students from any socioeconomic background the opportunity to learn valuable skills, and make a truly significant impact on the world through gobal and local technological innovation. We like to think of ourselves as the 'peace corps' of the tech industry.

Why?

Because we believe in people. Because we've been there. Because we know that people chasing capital gain will never make the same difference as people chasing change. Because we believe we can make a difference in the world with technology!
